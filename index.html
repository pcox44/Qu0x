Qu0x

Use each of the 5 dice exactly once with any combination of math operations (+, −, ×, ÷, parentheses) to get as close as possible to the target number.
A perfect score (0 difference) is called a Qu0x!
Your Current Qu0x Streak only increases when you earn a Qu0x.
